Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Regional Growth Conclave?

The Regional Growth Conclave is a premier event focused on urban development and smart city initiatives in Madhya Pradesh. It brings together government officials, urban planners, investors, and stakeholders to discuss and collaborate on the future of urban landscapes in the state.

How can I register for the event?

You can register for the event by clicking on the "RSVP Now" button on this website or by scanning the QR code provided. Registration is free but mandatory for all attendees.

Is there a dress code for the event?

Business formal attire is recommended for all attendees. The event is a professional gathering with government officials and industry leaders in attendance.

Will presentations be available after the event?

Yes, all presentations and materials from the conclave will be shared with registered attendees via email after the event. Make sure to provide your correct email address during registration.

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, Brilliant Convention Centre offers ample parking space for all attendees. Valet parking services will also be available.
